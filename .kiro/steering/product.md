# Product Overview

FitAgent is a multi-tenant SaaS platform that enables personal trainers to manage their business through an AI-powered WhatsApp assistant. The platform eliminates the need for traditional web or mobile applications by providing a conversational interface for both trainers and students.

## Core Value Proposition

Personal trainers can manage students, schedule training sessions, track payments, and send notifications entirely through WhatsApp conversations. Students interact with their trainers and view their schedules through the same natural language interface.

## Key Features

- Conversational AI interface using AWS Bedrock (Claude models)
- Multi-tenant architecture with isolated data per trainer
- Session scheduling with conflict detection
- Calendar integration (Google Calendar and Microsoft Outlook)
- Payment receipt tracking with S3 storage
- Automated session and payment reminders
- Broadcast notifications to students
- Phone number-based user identification and routing

## Target Users

- **Trainers**: Personal trainers who want to manage their business without complex software
- **Students**: Clients who prefer simple WhatsApp interactions over apps

## Business Model

Multi-tenant SaaS platform where each trainer operates independently with their own student base, while students can be linked to multiple trainers.
